How it works

A simple, predictable process — built around your deadline. From the first call to your final defence, you always know what’s next, what’s due, and what it costs.

1

Free 15-min discovery call

No cost · 15 minutes · video or phone

Tell us your research question, your timeline, and where you’re stuck. We’ll tell you — honestly — whether we’re the right fit, and what a typical engagement for a project like yours looks like.

  • What you bring: research question, deadline, supervisor expectations
  • What you leave with: a clear next step (or a referral if we’re not the right fit)
↓
2

Scoped, itemised quote

Within 48 hours · fixed-fee · no hourly surprises

We send a written quote that lists every deliverable — each table, figure, model and write-up — drawn from a published price catalogue. You and your supervisor see exactly what you’re paying for. A 50% deposit secures your slot.

  • Per-deliverable pricing (Table 1, regression model, methods section, etc.)
  • Fixed timeline with milestone dates
  • Quote valid for 30 days; first round of revisions on each item is included
↓
3

Analysis & iteration

1–4 weeks typical · reproducible R code · weekly check-ins

We clean the data, run the analyses, and build the tables and figures agreed in your quote. You see early drafts within the first week so we can course-correct before things are baked in. Everything ships with the underlying R code so your work is reproducible and defensible.

  • Reproducible R/Quarto workflow you can hand to your supervisor
  • Publication-ready tables and figures
  • Methods text drafted in parallel — paste-ready into your thesis or paper
↓
4

Deliver & defend

On-call support through review & defence

Final deliverables are packaged so they drop straight into your manuscript or thesis. We stay available for supervisor revisions, reviewer responses, and defence-prep questions — billed transparently in 30-minute blocks if needed.

  • Final code, data dictionaries, tables, figures and methods text
  • One round of revisions on each deliverable included free (within 14 days)
  • Reviewer-response support and defence prep available on request

What we ask of you

The fastest projects share three things: a clear research question, ethically-cleared data ready to share, and a responsive supervisor. If you’re not there yet, that’s fine — we often start with protocol or sample-size work and pick up the analysis once your data is in.

Our promises to you

Plain language

No statistical jargon you can’t defend yourself in a viva.

Reproducible by default

Every analysis ships with R code your supervisor can re-run.

No scope creep

If something changes, we re-quote before the work, not after.

Confidentiality

Your data, protocol and ideas stay yours. NDA on request.
